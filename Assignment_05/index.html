Ingredients

200 gr of shrimp tails

1 clove of garlic

50 ml of dry white wine

Extra virgin olive oil

350 ml of tomato sauce

100 ml of liquid cream

salt

1 sprig of parsley

Directions

1. Peel the clove of garlic and put it whole and crushed in a pan with a couple of tablespoons of oil.

2. Make it brown and then add the prawn tails, sauté for one minute, pour the wine and let it evaporate.

3. Add the tomato puree and salt and pepper to taste, continue to cook over a low heat for about twenty minutes.

4. Add the liquid cream and stir, bring to a boil and after a couple of minutes turn off the heat.

5. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain it al dente, sauté in a pan with the shrimp sauce and divide the portions into the dishes.

6. Finish with a handful of chopped parsley with your hands and serve.
